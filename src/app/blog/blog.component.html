Shopping for a new home involves a lot of important things. You’ve got to line up a realtor, sift through listings, narrow down your choices and set up viewings. Perhaps the most important step in purchasing a new home is getting mortgage loan preapproval. Doing so provides insight into the price range of homes you might qualify for. It also demonstrates to sellers that you’re serious about making a purchase.
