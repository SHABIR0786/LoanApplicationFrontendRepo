Loan Process

Curious about our loan process? These 7 steps will help summarize it for you!

Complete a Loan Application

Complete a short form loan application through our website, our talk to a licensed Mortgage Consultant directly by calling 1-(800) 930-8195

We Will Contact You

We Will Assign your loan application to a licensed Mortgage Consultant who will guide you through the loan process and serve as point of contact for questions and concerns. Your consultant will also:

  1. Review you loan application and answer questions about the process.
  2. Review your cradit history and submit your application through our automated underwritiong engine.
  3. Provide you with the “next step” in the loan process

Look an Interest Rate

If our automated underwriting engine issues an “Approved” decision (and you have received a purchase offer acceptance letter from the seller- if applicable) you are eligible to lock an interest rate online, or over the phone with your Mortgage Consultant.

If your loan does not recevie an “Approved” decision, your Mortgage Consultant will contact you to discuss additional options.

You may submit the deposite electronically through your online account or by calling your Mortgage Consultant.
