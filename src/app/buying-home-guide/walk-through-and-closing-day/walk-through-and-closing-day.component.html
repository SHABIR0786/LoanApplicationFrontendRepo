What to Look for in the Final Walk-Through

Before your official closing at the attorney or settlement agent’s office, you will meet at the house with your real estate agent to walk through for a visual inspection. The purpose is to make sure the house is in the same condition it was in when you entered the contract. You will also have a chance to see whether any repairs you requested have been completed to your satisfaction.

As a home buyer, you are about to make one of the biggest purchases of your life and you need to take your time to make sure the home hasn’t been damaged during the move-out process. Also, sometimes in the rush of moving, sellers can unintentionally leave things behind, so check attics, closets, and basements for forgotten items so they can be removed before you move in.
